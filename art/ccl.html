Choreographic Coding Lab 2018 and 2019

I had the opportunity to attend the Choreographic Coding Lab (CCL) in 2018 and 2019 in Mainz, Germany. CCLs are international workshops organized by Motion Bank that gather artists, designers, and researchers seeking to translate aspects of choreography and dance into digital form. I worked with motion capture data provided by Motion Bank to create self-generated visuals as well as edit motion data in real-time.
