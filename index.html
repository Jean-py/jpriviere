Hi!

I am a researcher in Human-Computer Interaction and I just finished my Ph.D. under the direction of Wendy Mackay, Sarah Fdili Alaoui and Baptiste Caramiaux.

I am particularly interested in understanding humans cognition and implicit knowledge. My approach is to design interactive tools usable in artistic practices, such as learning dance choreography. I am working with creative practitioners who push the limits of new technologies and our perception of the body. I am currently looking for a post-doctoral position combining the human body and interactive systems.
